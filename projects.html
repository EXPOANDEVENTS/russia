Metal-Expo'2025

The 31th International Industrial Exhibition
11-14 November 2025, Saint Petersburg, CEC Expoforum

Metal-Expo’2025 the largest in Russia and the CIS steel industry event will exhibit the whole range of ferrous and non-ferrous products, as well as the most state-of-the-art equipment and innovative solutions for the steel industry. Over its 30-year history Metal-Expo has become the main business platform for Russian, the CIS and East European steel producers and users. Metal-Expo and its events provide a perfect opportunity for steel industry professionals from all over the world to find new business solutions.
