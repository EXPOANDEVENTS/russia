Polymet on its participation in one of the key events of the foundry industry

Polymet once again took an active part in the Litmash 2025 exhibition, presenting its developments and products in the field of auxiliary materials for the foundry industry. The company proudly reaffirms its reputation as a reliable partner for enterprises across Russia, providing modern technological solutions and high-performance materials that contribute to optimizing foundry processes and improving product quality.

Over the three days of the exhibition, company specialists held numerous meetings and negotiations with representatives of foundries from all over Russia. Discussions included both current projects and potential areas of cooperation, strengthening and establishing new business contacts. These interactions also allowed for addressing key industry issues and identifying the actual needs of manufacturers, which can be met through Polymet’s technologies and materials. The company notes that they once again received interesting and important tasks, confirming the stable demand for their products.

Key outcomes of the company’s participation in the exhibition:

  • Presentation of innovative materials and solutions for the foundry industry

  • Effective negotiations with leading industry representatives

  • Confirmation of the company’s significant role in the development of the Russian foundry sector

The company's core areas of activity include:

  • Engineering consulting on foundry technologies, materials, and equipment

  • In-house production of:

    • technological lubricants and release agents

    • anti-stick coatings

    • binder systems

    • cleaning agents

  • Design of foundry production areas, including selection of appropriate equipment

  • Design and manufacturing of foundry tooling for:
    • sand casting
    • permanent mold casting
    • investment casting
    • lost foam casting

  • Consulting and services for refractory application, installation of linings in induction furnaces, ladles, heaters, and other equipment

  • Distribution and representation of leading foreign manufacturers of consumables and equipment for the foundry industry

The company expresses its sincere gratitude to all visitors and partners for their interest and trust.
